Our Story: Capturing the Value Chain

For too long, exceptional coffees like ours have seen most of their value captured by intermediaries. This venture is a strategic expansion to move beyond production and establish a direct-to-market presence in Europe. By controlling the high-margin retail and wholesale segments, we ensure the unique story and superior quality of our coffee reach the consumer intact.
